Welcome to Pizzatopia

Founded with passion and built on tradition, Pizzatopia is your go-to destination for handcrafted pizzas made with love and fresh ingredients.

Our Story

Pizzatopia opened its first store in Botswana on December 17, 2005, at Airport Junction Mall, Gaborone.Our founder, Chef Taliah, started this journey with one mission: to create the best pizza experience. With years of culinary expertise and a love for authentic flavors, she's built a menu that celebrates quality and taste.We use locally sourced ingredients, make our dough daily, and craft sauces from scratch — because quality matters in every bite.
